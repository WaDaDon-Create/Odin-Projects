Haitian Pate

Description

Haitian patty, also known as pâté haïtien or pate ayisyen in HaitianCreole is a small turnover of ground beef that is coated with puff pastry. The beef stuffing is seasoned with onions, garlic, shallots, but also lime, chili and aromatic herbs.

Ingredients

For the dough

  • 2½ cups all-purpose flour , sifted
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup water
  • 8 oz. lard (or, failing that, butter or margarine)
  • 1 egg yolk (to brush the dough)

For the Stuffing

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 medium onion , grated
  • 3 cloves garlic , chopped
  • 1 medium shallot , chopped
  • 1 teaspoon lime juice
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • ½ Scotch Bonnet pepper , chopped
  • ¼ teaspoon thyme
  • ¼ teaspoon rosemary
  • 2 teaspoons tomato paste
  • 3 teaspoons olive oil
  • ½ cup water

Instructions

  1. In a large saucepan, bring a large volume of water to a boil, and and salt.
  2. Add the spaghetti and cook until soft (about 10 minutes).
  3. Drain the spaghetti and set aside.
  4. In a large pot, add the olive oil, heat it and brown the sausage for 2 minutes
  5. Add the tomato paste, pepper, thyme, Scotch bonnet (or habanero pepper), onion and sauté for 3 minutes over medium heat, stirring frequently.
  6. Add 1 cup (250 ml) of water and bring to a boil.
  7. Add the cooked spaghetti and mix well.
  8. Lower the heat and simmer over very low heat for 5 minutes, stirring regularly.
  9. Serve hot.
